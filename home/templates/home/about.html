About Us

Our Mission

Our Mission is to advertise and sell exclusive sneakers to all members of the public. The unique market for individuals who have a taste in fashion. Owning one of a kind, limited edition, rare pair of sneakers will turn heads and having other individuals admiring your footwear. Giving users the different options of sneakers and sizes as well as being able to search for them to select from depending on availability.

Our History

Started following the trend for the new Jordans whenever they came out and having an addiction to Nike footwear, I found myself owning shoes that have no been worn. After being asked multiple times where have I purchased the pair of sneakers from, I was told they don't sell them anymore. 3 years later, I have been buying unique footwear and setting up an e-commerce site to allow other individuals to enjoy the unique style of the sneakers.
